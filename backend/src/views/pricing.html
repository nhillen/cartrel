Pricing FAQ

Why is Cartrel free for retailers?

Retailers are the network. Making Cartrel free for them encourages adoption and makes it easier for suppliers to invite partners. Suppliers capture most of the value, so they pay.

What about transaction fees or GMV percentage?

We're exploring usage-based models as an alternative to flat monthly fees, but will never charge traditional marketplace commissions (10-30%). Any usage pricing would be <1% with reasonable caps.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, all supplier plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What if I'm both a supplier and retailer?

You only pay for the supplier side. Your retailer features remain free.
